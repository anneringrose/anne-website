Fees

I offer a free 30 minute introductory session so that we can discuss what it is you want to work on and what you hope to achieve through counselling. After that sessions cost:

  • £45 per 60 minute session
  • £30 per 60 minute session for students

Concessions are available for some spaces. Please ask during the introductory session.

Payment can be made by BACS payment or cash and is due on the day of your appointment.

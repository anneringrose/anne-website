I am a BACP registered counsellor who is experienced in working with young people (11 years upwards) and adults both online and in person, near Dorchester.

We can all experience times of difficulty in our lives, when events or relationships feel overwhelming, or we can feel a loss of direction or purpose. I offer you a safe, confidential and non-judgemental space to explore what is going on for you and to find a way forward.

As I know that the relationship between the individual and the counsellor is the most important factor in successful counselling, I offer a free 30 minute meeting online so that you can see if you feel comfortable working with me before making any commitment.

I am now available for both face-to-face and online sessions or a blending of both if that is helpful.
